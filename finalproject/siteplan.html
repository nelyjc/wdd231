Site Plan: Shaded Playgrounds

Site Name

Shaded Playgrounds

This site name reflects its purpose clearly — helping parents and caregivers find playgrounds that offer shade, making outdoor play more comfortable and safe for children. It's simple, descriptive, and easy to remember.

domain availability: shadedplaygrounds.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to help families discover shaded playgrounds nearby. It includes an interactive map, user-submitted reviews, park features (like shade structures, splash pads, or restrooms), and a form to suggest new shaded playgrounds.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find shaded playgrounds near me?
  • Are there any parks with both shade and water play for hot days?

Color Schema

  • Forest Green (#2c5d63): Used for all headings, borders, and the site’s navigation background.
  • Light Sand (#fdfaf6): Used as the overall page background to give a soft, clean look.
  • Golden Yellow (#f4a261): Used for buttons, highlights, and important callouts.

Typography

  • Raleway Used for headers and navigation text for a modern, friendly style.
  • Open Sans Used for body text to ensure readability and accessibility across all devices.
